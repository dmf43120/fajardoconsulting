01

The Clarity Audit

See what's actually broken.

A focused 3–4 week engagement where we go deep on your operations, systems, and processes — and deliver a prioritized, executive-ready plan you can act on immediately. This is your entry point: low risk, high clarity, and a clear foundation for what comes next.

Ideal For
Companies that sense something is off operationally but lack the visibility to articulate exactly what. Also the right starting point for any scaling company that wants an objective operational assessment before making major system or team investments.

Embedded, ongoing execution.

You get a part-time Head of Operations — embedded in your team, running your roadmap, building your systems, and giving your leadership the operational clarity they need. Without the cost or commitment of a full-time executive hire. Monthly retainer, 3-month minimum.

Ideal For
