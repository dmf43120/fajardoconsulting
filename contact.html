Common Questions

Before you reach out.

How quickly can you start?
Typically within 1–2 weeks of a signed agreement and initial payment. I keep a limited number of active engagements to ensure every client gets full attention and fast turnaround.
Do I need to have everything figured out before reaching out?
Absolutely not. Most clients reach out knowing something is wrong but not exactly what. That's exactly what the Clarity Audit is designed for. The discovery call itself helps clarify what you need.
What industries do you work with?
Operational problems are industry-agnostic. Deep background in fintech, SaaS, and platform operations — but the operational principles apply across scaling companies in any sector.
Can you work with us if we're not in Phoenix?
Yes. FCG is remote-first. Every engagement can be conducted fully remotely. I work with companies nationwide and the vast majority of operational work doesn't require an on-site presence.
What if we start with an audit but want to continue working together?
That's the most common path. The Clarity Audit almost always surfaces enough work to warrant a Fractional Ops Partner engagement to execute the roadmap. About 70% of audit clients continue to a retainer.
How do you handle confidentiality?
Every engagement includes a comprehensive confidentiality agreement. Client information, processes, and data are never shared. Any published case studies are fully anonymized and require your explicit approval.
